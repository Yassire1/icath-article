Time Series Foundation Models (TSFMs) promise zero-shot generalization across domains, yet their efficacy in industrial Predictive Maintenance (PdM) remains largely uncharacterized. We benchmark three leading open-source TSFMs (MOMENT, Chronos, Lag-Llama) against the supervised PatchTST baseline across three public PdM datasets spanning wind turbines, turbofan engines, and CNC tool wear monitoring. We evaluate zero-shot forecasting performance and cross-condition transfer on turbofan degradation data, and we complement these experiments with deployment-oriented efficiency measurements.

\textbf{Key contributions:} (1) An empirical comparison of zero-shot TSFM performance on industrial sensor data; (2) A cross-condition transfer evaluation measuring how well TSFMs generalize across different operating conditions within the same domain; (3) Practical deployment guidelines quantifying model efficiency trade-offs (parameters, VRAM, inference latency) relevant for edge and cloud PdM deployments. Code and datasets available at: \url{https://github.com/YassireF/tsfm-industrial-bench}.
